About Us

Why we built this and who it's for

The Belief

Every person — no matter how poor, how young, or how forgotten — has something special inside them. A talent. A gift. A dream that deserves to be seen.

But most of these people will never get discovered. Not because they're not good enough — but because nobody is looking.

MyTalent.MyDream exists to change that.

What We Do

We build free websites for talented people who can't afford one — especially kids and underprivileged communities in Cebu and the Philippines.

  • We find people with hidden talents in communities
  • We build them their own website page — for free
  • We share their story so the world can find them
  • We connect them with people who want to help

No fees. No catches. Just belief in people.

Who Can Submit

Anyone. If you have a talent or know someone who does — especially someone who doesn't have the platform or resources to be seen — submit it. We'll do the rest.

  • Kids who sing in the streets
  • Teens who draw but can't afford art school
  • Mothers who craft beautiful things at home
  • Anyone with a gift and a dream

How You Can Help

You don't need money to make a difference. Here's how:

  • Share — Share a talent profile on Facebook. One share can reach the right person.
  • Submit — Know someone talented? Submit their story.
  • Connect — If you can offer training, mentorship, equipment, or opportunities — reach out directly.
  • Sponsor — Want to sponsor a talent's dream? Contact us.

Behind This Project

MyTalent.MyDream was created in Cebu, Philippines by someone who believes that the most powerful thing you can give a person isn't money — it's the belief that they matter.

This platform is built with love and maintained for free. Because some things are more important than profit.
